BLOCK 1 – APTITUDE (Speed, Time & Distance)

Question 1
A train travels 60 km in 1 hour. What is its speed in m/s?

	Speed = 60 km/h
	Convert km/h to m/s
	= 60 × 5/18
	= 300/18
	= 16.67 m/s


Question 2
A car travels at 40 km/h for 2 hours and 60 km/h for next 2 hours.
Find average speed.

	Distance1 = 40 × 2 = 80 km
	Distance2 = 60 × 2 = 120 km
	Total distance = 80 + 120 = 200 km
	Total time = 2 + 2 = 4 hours
	Average speed = Total distance / Total time
              = 200 / 4
              = 50 km/h


Question 3
Two trains moving in opposite directions at 50 km/h and 70 km/h.
Find relative speed.

	Relative speed = 50 + 70
               = 120 km/h


Question 4
A person walks at 5 km/h and runs at 10 km/h.
Find time taken to cover 15 km if half distance is walked and half is run.

	Half distance = 15/2 = 7.5 km

	Walking  = 7.5 / 5 
	         = 1.5 hours
	Running time = 7.5 / 10 
		     = 0.75 hours
	Total time = 1.5 + 0.75
           	   = 2.25 hours
	(2 hours 15 minutes)

Question 5
If a train crosses a pole in 10 seconds and its length is 100 meters,
find its speed.

	Speed = Distance / Time
      = 100 / 10
      = 10 m/s
	Convert to m/s to km/hr
	= 10 × 18/5
	= 36 km/h
